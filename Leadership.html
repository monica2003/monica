Fremont Mental Health Organization

Treasurer
Sept 2018 - Present

Fremont Mental Health Organization is a group of high school students from the Bay Area working together to help improve the public image of mental health issues. We host informational and community events to reduce the stigma around mental health.

In the past, FMHO has done many events and service projects. For our first event, we held a panel where we brought in mental health professionals to speak about dealing with high schoool stress. Our most recent service project is depicted in the pictures. We make over fifty care packages for the homeless shelter as well as many other donations.
